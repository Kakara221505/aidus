You could transfer your digital assets to your private deposit address or alternatively, you could top up from your MetaMask wallet. For transferring from your external wallet or an exchange, please follow the steps below:
1. Log in to your AIDUS account.
2. Complete Identity Verification if you haven't done it already. Don't worry, the process is very quick and intuitive. and choose the digital assets and enter the amount.
3. Choose 'Wallet Transfer' and scan QR code or copy your private deposit address.
4. Paste your deposit address in the "send to/withdrawal address" field on the platform/wallet from which you will transfer your assets to your AIDUS account.
5. You will see your crypto deposit in your AIDUS account as soon as the necessary amount of network confirmations is reached.

By using the web browser version of the Aidus platform, you can now top up crypto assets directly from your MetaMask account. To initiate a transfer, you would first need to connect your MetaMask wallet to your Aidus account. If you do not have it installed, follow their website's steps. Once you have it up and running, you connect it to our platform following these straightforward steps:
1. Log in to your AIDUS account.
2. Complete Identity Verification if you haven't done it already. Don't worry, the process is very quick and intuitive. and choose the digital assets and enter the amount.
3. Choose 'Via Metamask' and once you enter the desired amount and click the ‘Top Up’ button, the MetaMask extension pop-up will open in the top-right corner of your browser. On the MetaMask extension screen, you will see the asset amount you have available for transfer from the wallet you have selected.
4. On the MetaMask extension screen you will be able to review the transaction details. If everything is in order, scroll to the bottom of the pop-up screen and click ‘Confirm’.
5. Once you confirm your top-up, you will receive confirmation of the successfully executed transfer. Additionally, you will be able to cancel or monitor the status of your transaction from the MetaMask extension. Just click on the MetaMask extension icon on the top-right corner of your browser window and the MetaMask pop-up will open.
