4. COOKIES, BEACONS AND SIMILAR TECHNOLOGIES

The Company may collect aggregated, non-personal information through cookies or web beacons.

A cookie is a small piece of data sent by the Company's web server to the user's browser and stored on the user computer's hard disk.

Web beacons are small strings of code that are placed on a website or in an email. They allow checking that a user has interacted with specific web or email content. If cookie technology is not available, as in mobile applications, we may also use technology that performs similar functions to cookies (for example, advertising identifiers).

These features are used to customize the user experience by evaluating and improving the services and to enable us to provide better services for our users.
